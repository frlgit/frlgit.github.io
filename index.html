NY Elites Cricket League

The NY Elites Cricket League brings together Albany's finest cricketing talent on a professionally organized and competitive platform. Established in 2025, the league is dedicated to promoting high-quality cricket, sportsmanship, and community growth. With a structured format, and a focus on fair play, NY Elites aims to create an exciting and rewarding experience for players and teams across the region.
